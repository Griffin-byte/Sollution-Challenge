🔒 Privacy Policy - Secret Talk 🔒

Last Updated: 24/02/2025

1. Information We Collect

Since **Secret Talk is an anonymous platform**, we **do not** require login/signup. However, we collect limited data to ensure platform functionality:

  • 🔹 **Anonymous confessions & chat messages (stored temporarily in Firebase)
  • 🔹 **User mood data** (stored temporarily for AI chatbot personalization)
  • 🔹 **IP address & device info** (for security & moderation purposes, stored temporarily)
  • 🔹 **User interactions** (how you navigate and use the platform, stored for analytics)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this data to:

  • ✔ Improve chatbot responses by analyzing recent conversations
  • ✔ Ensure user safety by detecting policy violations
  • ✔ Provide a **personalized chatbot experience**
  • ✔ Prevent spam, abuse, or misuse

3. Data Retention & Deletion

Since **Secret Talk does not permanently store user data**, all messages and confessions:

  • ⏳ Are **automatically deleted** from Firebase **within 1-2 days**
  • 📌 Are **not linked to any personal identity**
  • ❌ Users can **request immediate data removal** by contacting support

4. AI Chatbot & User Privacy

Our **AI chatbot (Gemini API)** customizes responses based on recent conversations. To protect privacy:

  • 🤖 Chat history is **stored temporarily** (1-2 days) for better AI interaction
  • 🚀 The AI does **not learn** or store past data permanently
  • 🔒 Sensitive words/topics are **filtered to ensure a safe experience**

5. Sharing & Third-Party Access

We **DO NOT** share user data with third parties. However, we may:

  • 📌 Use **Firebase for real-time data storage**
  • 📌 Use **Google Gemini API** for chatbot responses
  • 📌 Share **anonymized data** (no personal info) for analytics or platform improvement

6. Security Measures

To keep your data safe, we use:

  • 🔐 **End-to-end encryption** for chatbot messages
  • 🛡 **Real-time moderation** to detect and remove harmful content
  • 🚨 **IP tracking (only for security reasons)** to prevent spam & abuse

7. User Rights

As a **Secret Talk user**, you have the right to:

  • 📌 Request **immediate deletion** of any confession/chat
  • 📌 **Opt-out of AI-based personalization** (by disabling mood detection)
  • 📌 Report privacy concerns & inappropriate content

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Any significant changes will be **notified via a site announcement**.

9. Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or want to request data removal, contact us at:

📩 support@secrettalk.com

🔒 Your privacy is our priority! 🔒
